About Community Food Handler

Making a difference in our community through safe food handling and distribution

Our Mission

At Community Food Handler, we believe that no food should go to waste while people in our community go hungry. Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need through safe, efficient, and dignified food distribution practices.

What We Do

  • Collect surplus food from local businesses, restaurants, and farms
  • Ensure all food meets safety standards through rigorous inspection
  • Distribute food to individuals and families in need
  • Provide food safety education and training
  • Partner with local organizations to maximize impact

Our Impact

50,000+ Pounds of food rescued
2,500+ Families served
150+ Volunteers
